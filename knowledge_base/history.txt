History: Focuses on the Mughal Empire and Indian Freedom Struggle as per Tamil Nadu Samacheer Kalvi Class 12 syllabus. Shah Jahan, the fifth Mughal emperor, ruled from 1628 to 1658. He commissioned the Taj Mahal, a white marble mausoleum in Agra, for his wife Mumtaz Mahal, who died in 1631. The Indian Freedom Struggle began with the 1857 Revolt, also known as the First War of Independence. It gained momentum with leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, who led movements such as the Dandi Salt March in 1930 to protest British salt taxes.
