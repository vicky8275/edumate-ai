Civics (Political Science): Focuses on Indian Constitution and Governance as per Tamil Nadu Samacheer Kalvi Class 12 syllabus. The Indian Constitution, adopted in 1950, establishes India as a sovereign, socialist, secular, democratic republic. Fundamental Rights include the Right to Equality (Article 14). The President of India is the head of state, while the Prime Minister leads the government.

Worldwide Knowledge: India’s Constitution draws from global models, like the U.S. Constitution’s judicial review and the U.K.’s parliamentary system. Concepts like democracy and secularism are universal, seen in nations like France (secularism) and the U.S. (democracy).
